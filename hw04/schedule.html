* The Oktoberfest celebration is ongoing from September 16th to October 3rd. Event hours are modified for certain dates of the celebration. Please see below.

The first day, September 16th:

  • Beer tents open at 9 AM, serving non-alcoholic beverages at 10 AM, then serving alcohol at noon. Tents close at 11:30PM.
  • The stalls open at 9 AM and close at midnight.
  • Fairground rides and showmen open at noon and close at midnight.

Monday, October 2nd:

  • Beer tents are open and serving alcohol during regular Monday hours.
  • The stalls open at 10 AM AM and close at midnight.
  • Fairground rides and showmen open at 10 AM and close at midnight.

The Holiday, October 3rd:

  • The beer tents are open and serving alcohol from 9 AM to 11:30 PM.
  • The stalls open at 9 AM and close at 11:30 PM.
  • Fairground rides and showmen functioning during regular Tuesday hours.
